Case Study Details

The Challenge

The client, a Series B tech startup specializing in AI solutions, needed to triple their engineering team within 3 months to meet product development deadlines. Their unique culture of innovation and collaboration made finding the right talent particularly challenging.

Key challenges included:

  • Highly competitive market for AI/ML engineers
  • Need for candidates who fit both technical requirements and company culture
  • Aggressive timeline that couldn't compromise hiring quality

Our Solution

Prime Talent implemented a multi-pronged approach:

  1. Customized Sourcing Strategy: Targeted passive candidates through niche platforms and community engagement
  2. Technical Assessment Redesign: Created role-specific evaluations that mirrored real work challenges
  3. Cultural Fit Framework: Developed a structured interview process to assess cultural alignment
  4. Candidate Experience Focus: Streamlined communication and feedback loops to keep candidates engaged
